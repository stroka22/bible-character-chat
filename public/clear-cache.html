Browser Cache Clearing Instructions

If you're still experiencing issues, try clearing your browser cache manually:

Chrome
  • Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  • Set time range to "All time"
  • Check "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files"
  • Click "Clear data"
  • Restart Chrome
Firefox
  • Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  • Set time range to "Everything"
  • Check "Cookies" and "Cache"
  • Click "Clear Now"
  • Restart Firefox
Safari
  • Go to Safari menu > Preferences > Advanced
  • Enable "Show Develop menu in menu bar"
  • Go to Develop menu > Empty Caches
  • Go to Safari menu > Clear History
  • Choose "all history" and click "Clear History"
  • Restart Safari
Edge
  • Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete
  • Set time range to "All time"
  • Check "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files"
  • Click "Clear now"
  • Restart Edge
